About Andrea Flowers Shop Slovakia

Recognised as one of the best florists near Dunajská Streda, Andrea Flowers combines passion and expertise to bring elegance to every floral design. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a beloved Andrea Flowers shop in Slovakia. Learn more about our story and why locals trust us for all their floral needs.

History of the shop

Founded in 1998, Andrea Flowers has blossomed from a small local boutique into one of the community's most beloved floral destinations. What began as a dream by Andrea herself—armed with a passion for color, fragrance, and natural beauty—has grown into a family-run shop known for personal service and exquisite floral arrangements.

Visit Our Store and Meet the Team

At Andrea Flowers, our passionate florists bring creativity and care to every bouquet. Each team member brings years of floral design experience and a personal love for crafting meaningful arrangements.

Nestled in the heart of Ekecs, Slovakia, our shop is filled with fresh blooms daily, curated by our dedicated team who believe that flowers aren’t just gifts—they’re stories, celebrations, and memories in bloom. Whether you're marking a milestone or simply brightening someone's day, Andrea Flowers brings your sentiment to life with elegance and care.
